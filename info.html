Designer based in Watertown, Massachussetts

Currently Amanda is the Lead Designer at Albert, an app that acts as your financial genius and helps you save effortlessly. Previously she was an Imagineer at Walt Disney Imagineering—the birthplace of Disneyland (and all Disney theme parks)—where she visualized business data and contributed as a type designer

In her free time Amanda moonlights as a freelance designer, helping small businesses with visual branding and web development. When she's not working, she enjoys spending time with her dog, Captain.
